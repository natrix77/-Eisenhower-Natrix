What is the Eisenhower Matrix?

The Eisenhower Matrix is a powerful productivity tool that helps you prioritize tasks based on their urgency and importance. It divides your tasks into four categories:

Urgent & Important

Tasks that need immediate attention

Not Urgent & Important

Tasks that are important but can be scheduled

Urgent & Not Important

Tasks that are urgent but can be delegated

Not Urgent & Not Important

Tasks that can be eliminated or minimized
